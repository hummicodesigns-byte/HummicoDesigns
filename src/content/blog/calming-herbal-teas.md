There's a reason a warm cup of herbal tea has been part of evening routines for generations. Beyond the flavour, it's the ritual the pause, the warmth, the few quiet minutes that are entirely your own. Here are some of the botanicals people reach for most often, and the blends we weave them into at Hummico.

## A cup as a small ritual

Herbal teas (or tisanes) are caffeine-free infusions made from flowers, leaves, roots and fruit rather than the tea plant. That makes them a gentle companion for the late afternoon and evening, when many people like to slow down. None of them are a remedy, they're simply a comforting, fragrant way to mark a moment.

## Chamomile — the classic evening cup

Soft, golden and lightly honeyed, chamomile is the herb most people picture when they think "calming tea." It has been traditionally enjoyed in the evening for centuries, and its mellow, apple-like flavour makes it an easy favourite. You'll find it at the heart of several of our gentle herbal blends.

## Lavender — quiet and floral

Lavender brings a distinctive, perfumed note that many people find lovely to sip and to breathe in. A little goes a long way, so we pair it carefully with softer botanicals to keep the floral character elegant rather than overpowering.

## Rose — gentle and romantic

Rose petals add a delicate, fragrant sweetness and a beautiful look to any blend. Long loved across Persian and Middle Eastern tea traditions, rose is the star of our floral herbal blends - chosen to feel like a slow, fragrant garden walk in a cup.

## Peppermint — bright and refreshing

If chamomile sits at the cosy end of the spectrum, peppermint is the crisp, reviving one. Its clean, cooling flavour is wonderful after a meal and equally lovely on its own. Peppermint and spearmint also anchor our minty herbal blends, inspired by the generous, sweet mint tea traditionally served to welcome guests.

## Hibiscus & rooibos — ruby-red and caffeine-free

For something a little different, hibiscus brings a tart, cranberry-like brightness and a gorgeous ruby colour, while rooibos (red bush) offers a naturally sweet, earthy base that's just as good iced as hot. Both are caffeine-free and easy to enjoy at any time of day.

## Our blends, brought together

We design each Hummico blend to tell a little story — some rose-forward and floral, some bright with sweet mint, others built around gentle chamomile and soft florals.

Each one is hand-blended and packed in small batches here in Houston, so the botanicals arrive fragrant and fresh.

## Finding your favourite

The best calming tea is simply the one you most look forward to. Try a floral blend one evening and a minty one the next; brew a little stronger or softer until it's exactly right for you.
