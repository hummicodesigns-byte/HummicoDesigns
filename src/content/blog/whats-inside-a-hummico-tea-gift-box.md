A Hummico tea gift box is designed to feel like a moment of calm wrapped up and handed to someone you care about. So what's actually inside? Here's a look at the kinds of thoughtful pieces we tuck into our boxes — and how to choose the right one.

## More than tea

While beautiful tea is always the centrepiece, our boxes are curated as a complete little ritual. The idea is that everything works together: something to brew, something sweet, and a few gentle touches that turn an ordinary afternoon into a treat.

## The teas & botanical blends

At the heart of every box is a selection of our hand-blended, loose-leaf botanicals. Depending on the box, that might include:

- **Persian Herbal Garden** — a rose-forward, floral blend
- **Moroccan** — bright, sweet mint
- **English Garden** — chamomile and soft florals
- Single botanicals such as chamomile, lavender, peppermint, hibiscus and rooibos
